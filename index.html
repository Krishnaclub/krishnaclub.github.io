About Us

Welcome to the official website of Shree Krishna Dramatic Club, Mullana. Since 1928, we have been bringing the story of Lord Ram to life every year through our grand Ramleela performance.

Ramleela 2025 Schedule

From 18th September to 2nd October (Dussehra)

  • 18th Sept: (Shravan Leela – Ram Janm)
  • 19th Sept: (Tadka Vadh)
  • 20th Sept: (Ram-Sita Vivah)
  • 21st Sept: (Kaikayi's Demands and Exile)
  • 22nd Sept: (Dashrath's Death)
  • 23rd Sept:(Bharat Milap)
  • 24th Sept: (Sawroop nakha ki naak aur khar dushan vadh)
  • 25th Sept: (Sita Haran)
  • 26th Sept: (Bali vadh)
  • 27th Sept: (Lanka Dahan)
  • 28th Sept: (Angad's Foot)
  • 29th Sept: (Lakshman Murcha)
  • 30th Sept: (Megnadh and Kumbhkaran's Death)
  • 1st Oct: (Ravan’s Last Battle)
  • 2nd Oct: (Dussehra – Ravan Dahan)
